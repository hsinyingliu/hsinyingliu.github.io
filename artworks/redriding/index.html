Red Riding Hood

marker pen,
oil pencil on cardboard

This illustration is rather close to my heart as it incorporates lots of sentimental details and vivid memories from my early childhood, lots of apparently unrelated or insignificant knick-knacks, in terms of that, they all connect to each other into some kind of time machine to my own past.

HeySong is an unique sort of fizzy drink that all the children crave for. The soft drink can be bought in a traditional Taiwanese store which is mostly located in the suburb areas of Taiwan. My Grandmother used to take me there and just let me linger around and then pick up my favorite carbonated beverage.
