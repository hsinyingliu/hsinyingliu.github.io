Artworks About Resume

Switch

animation (2009)
(group project)

The project Switch was my graduation group project at Kun-Shan Unversity, my role in the project has been of the sole illustrator.

The plot of the animation is about a naughty little boy who grow up in a wealthy family, and who likes to torture animals. The more suffering he inflicts, the joyful he is.

The story was chosen as a fable to some human beings who are having this kind of behaviour in our society.

The animation has won several awards, including the 3rd place at the Young Designers' Exhibition, Taiwan, a national exhibition of all the graduates.
