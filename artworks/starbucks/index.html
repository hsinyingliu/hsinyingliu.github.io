Starbucks Coffee

pencil

Even though greed is considered a sin by most of us, sometimes you can't help having that feeling, and you know deep inside you that it's not right, it's in your human nature.

Even worse is the situation when you strongly believe that the other person doesn't even deserve the thing you aspire to, and all you can think about is how unfair the whole situation is, and wish you could change it.

I've made the drawing to capture this exact idea, as I have encountered many people in my life that had this thought at some point.
