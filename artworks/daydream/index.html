Daydream

marker pen,
oil pencil on cardboard

This is another personal artwork I have done in a blissful moment of my life. While I imagined sinking in my couch with a bowl of mouth-watering dessert, simply enjoying my favorite TV program.As if the mildy beautiful weather outside sneaked into my room, I was over the moon..

This personal drawing allows me to truly explore my imagination and let the kid emerges from me which appears on the piece of paper.
