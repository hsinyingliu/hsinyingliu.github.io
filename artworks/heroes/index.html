Heroes and Superheroes

vector illustration

I grow up among tons of video games and cartoons and I remember so many of the characters, as I enjoyed them immensely. I used to sit on the carpet in front of the TV, having some snacks with my two older brothers and being mesmerized by all the elements.

Each character represents its own stories and reminds me of different stage of my childhood. Behind all of these characters I have preserved some valuable memories of mine. That's why I have placed all of them together, like they all live together in a close-knit village.
